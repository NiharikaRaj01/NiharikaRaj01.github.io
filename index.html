Projects

Compounding Algorithm for 3D ultrasound reconstruction using Freehand 2D acquisition.

This project focuses on developing an advanced 3D ultrasound reconstruction algorithm aimed at reducing artifacts and enhancing image quality, thereby improving the clinical utility of ultrasound imaging. The primary objectives include minimizing common artifacts in ultrasound images to achieve clearer, more accurate representations, and employing optimized compounding methods to produce high-quality 3D images.

The proof of concept involves simulating a real-time, wearable ultrasound machine specifically designed to scan the thyroid gland. This innovative prototype will demonstrate the algorithm's effectiveness in real-world scenarios, showcasing its capability for continuous monitoring and immediate imaging feedback.
